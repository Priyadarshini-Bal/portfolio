I envy the passing cloud

I envy the passing cloud
moving around
spreading their wings
on the limitless sky
while here I staggered with
the weight of
unsaid words calling
for my attention
unhealed scars demand
to be caressed
incomplete dreams still
banging on my door
should I watch them
giving up on me
or treated them as my equal?

2022

January taught me about a new dawn,
February told me...life would be great if I had patience,
March had been screaming dreams and passion as quarter months have gone,
April sang me a lullaby of gratitude, which I often forget to say aloud.
May asked me whether I'm content and happy with the past months, I simply shrugged and grinned.
June kindly stands by me when I'm still waiting with mighty quill and blank pages, where have my muse, moon and stars vanished to?

Ignorance and Time

Time is ignorant of love,
and love is of time,
eyes value poets who do
not know how to rhyme.
Ears hunger for music
that settels the mind.
Moons are ignorant of the skies
that are blue all the time.
The sun does not know there are
other stars that shines even
after the end of the time.
